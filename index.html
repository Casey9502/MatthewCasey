Audio Production Skills

My background in audio is grounded in hands-on experience with recording, mixing, and mastering across diverse genres. I specialize in shaping sound to serve emotion and narrative—whether for music, film, or interactive media.

I work confidently in Pro Tools, Ableton Live, Reaper, and Logic Pro. My toolkit also includes iZotope RX for audio restoration, Max/MSP for creative coding, and DAWs integrated into Unity for game development. Alongside technical fluency, I bring collaborative energy, clear communication, and a strong commitment to deadlines.
